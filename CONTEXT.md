# Portal Messenger

Portal Messenger is a playful realtime social product that presents a communal online office through the visual language of early-2000s desktop software.

## Language

**Portal Messenger**:
The public-facing chat application, formally presented as "Portal Messenger: Corporate Edition."
_Avoid_: Slack XP, Slack clone, Windows Messenger

**Portal Systems**:
The fictional dysfunctional software company whose workplace contains the Shared Public Office.
_Avoid_: Portal tenant, customer organization, real employer

**Shared Public Office**:
The single communal workspace in the hosted product, where New Hires meet and participate together.
_Avoid_: Slack clone, tenant, organization, private workspace

**New Hire**:
An authenticated person participating in the Shared Public Office, framed by the product as newly employed at its fictional 2001-era software company.
_Avoid_: Visitor, employee, member, end user

**New Hire Profile**:
The current public name and picture a New Hire has chosen through Clerk and presents throughout the Shared Public Office.
_Avoid_: Employee identity, Portal user, message author snapshot

**Former Employee**:
The anonymous public identity shown for the preserved messages of a deleted Clerk account.
_Avoid_: Deleted user, deactivated profile, unknown user

**Observer**:
An unauthenticated person viewing live Office Channel messages without permission to send messages.
_Avoid_: Guest, anonymous Portal user, lurker

**Office Channel**:
One of the fixed, product-curated conversation spaces in the Shared Public Office, with its own workplace theme and purpose.
_Avoid_: User-created channel, room, group

**System Event**:
A product-authored message that turns realtime activity or office conditions into an in-world early-2000s workplace joke.
_Avoid_: Bot message, notification, announcement

**Office Event**:
A realtime occurrence that updates the office experience without appearing as an ordinary conversation message.
_Avoid_: Control message, socket event, internal event

**Removed Message**:
A message an operator has removed from the Portal Messenger experience while preserving its place in the conversation.
_Avoid_: Portal retraction, hard-deleted message

**Send Home**:
An operator ban that removes a New Hire from the current Office Day and expires at its UTC boundary.
_Avoid_: Temporary ban, timeout, suspension

**Termination**:
An operator ban that prevents a New Hire from entering future Office Days until an operator reverses it.
_Avoid_: Permanent ban, account deletion, deactivation

**HR Report**:
A private request from a New Hire for an operator to review a message or New Hire Profile and choose an action or dismiss it.
_Avoid_: Flag, complaint, moderation event

**Operator**:
A trusted New Hire authorized to review HR Reports, remove messages, and apply or reverse employment actions.
_Avoid_: Admin user, moderator, workspace owner

**Office Character**:
A clearly labeled fictional Portal Systems persona that authors scripted System Events without being presented as a real New Hire.
_Avoid_: Fake user, AI coworker, simulated employee

**Office Day**:
One daily edition of the Shared Public Office, with its own visible Office Channel history and a fresh start at the next daily boundary.
_Avoid_: Session, retention window, workspace reset
